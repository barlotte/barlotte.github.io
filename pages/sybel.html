From Product Manager (Jul. 19 - Sep. 20) to Chief Product Officer (Sep. 20 - Apr. 23)

Sybel is a premium streaming platform for podcasts and audio series, renowned for producing and licensing high-quality audio content, including series, podcasts, and audiobooks.

My role encompassed guiding the product's lifecycle in its evolution from free model to full monetization, international expansion, and optimizing for key metrics across all its verticals ensuring user satisfaction and growth.

  • Strategic planning: roadmapping and features prioritization
  • Continuous Product Discovery: leveragging product research activities, user insights and product data
  • Delivery Management : backlog management, coordinate release management of the cross-platform experience
  • Product Operations: continuous improvement for both product discovery and delivery processes and tooling
  • Communication: leverage interpersonal skills to ensure stakeholders alignment, create resources and tooling for an open communication across all departments (Tech, Marketing, Content, and Legal)
  • Management of a team of 7 tech and product people

  • Guided transition to premium model: implementation and daily management of multi-platform subscription system (on app stores, stripe, telco & partners integration), continuous improvement of monetization strategies, AB/testing
  • Led the Internationalization of the product and localization of our apps & library in French, English, and Spanish
  • Launched key projects including an Advent Calendar Skill with Alexa France, a new TV app on Freebox, and an in-car app on Android Auto with Renault.
