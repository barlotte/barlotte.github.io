Sybel is a premium streaming platform for podcasts and audio series, renowned for producing and licensing high-quality audio content, including series, podcasts, and audiobooks.

During nearly 4 years directing the product department at Sybel, some key aspects of my role included:

  • Pairing with CTO and leading a team of 6 developers and 1 designer.
  • Overseeing the product's lifecycle and transitionning from MVP stage to 1M+ Active Users.
  • Handling challenges ranging from monetization strategies to app internationalization, consistently emphasizing user experience and better content recommendations.
  • Being responsible for the Product data management and analysis, helping the adoption of tools such as Segment and Amplitude within the company, catering to the needs of Marketing and Editorial teams for informed decision-making.
  • Maintaining open communication across all departments (Tech, Marketing, Content, and Legal).

We primarily focused on mobile platforms (iOS and Android), winning the title of Best App of the Year by Google Play Store in 2019. Our partnerships with entities like Alexa France, SFR, and Renault empowered us to expand our footprint, offering a holistic cross-platform experience including Web, TV, Voice Assistants, and in-car experience.
