Frak enables users to earn rewards for consuming content on platforms such as YouTube and facilitates their investment in creators. On the other side, creators unlock an additionnal revenue stream by minting their content, earning rewards each time their content is consumed.

My role was to research, and deliver the first versions of Frak platform:

  • Research: Conducted in-depth research to identify user needs and market gaps, laying the groundwork for the platform's direction.
  • Design: Crafted the blueprint for the Frak platform, ensuring a seamless interface for users. This included a comprehensive dashboard for creators to mint their content on the blockchain, and a Chrome Extension allowing $FRK earnings when end-users consume the content on YouTube.
  • UX: Optimized the user journey, especially on the Chrome Extension. This extension transforms YouTube watch time into $FRK, Frak's proprietary cryptocurrency. Key features I designed included a wallet balance claim, currency swapping, content investment options, and a dedicated shop for users to redeem currency on cultural products and services.
